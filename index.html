My journey into tech began with a devotion to literature. After graduating from UT Austin, I wove that love for words into producing events and designing custom print materials. I found myself wanting to outsource less, and create more. Learning Photoshop helped me to brand events from scratch, and I found myself ready to move on from typical drag + drop sites like squarespace. With coding, I’ve discovered a fluid, adaptable language that I’m eager to use, to build custom, gorgeous and intuitive web experiences.
